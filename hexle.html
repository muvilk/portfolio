Project Hexle

Hexle: Unlimited is a game that takes inspiration from classic code-breaking puzzles and injects a fresh layer of complexity with hexadecimal digits (0-F). Players test their logic and deduction skills by cracking color-coded clues to guess the correct hex number with unlimited attempts. In Hexle, the thrill lies in cracking the code with the fewest attempts possible. Compete against friends to climb the leaderboard by achieving the lowest number of guesses. With each attempt, hone your skills and aim for the top spot. Will you outsmart your friends and emerge as the ultimate Hexle champion?
