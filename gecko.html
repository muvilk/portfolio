Project Gecko: Your Ultimate Emissions Management Solution

GECkO, the Emissions Management System (EMS) app, empowers companies to accurately track, report, and reduce carbon emissions across their supply chains.

Key Features

  • Real-Time Emissions Tracking: Track carbon emissions data across your supply chain with real-time updates.
  • Comprehensive Reporting: Generate detailed reports on emissions across various metrics for better insight and decision-making.
  • Data Visualization: View emissions data through visually interactive dashboards for a clearer understanding of sustainability efforts.
  • Collaboration Tools: Share best practices, success stories, and collaborate with other companies to meet sustainability goals.
