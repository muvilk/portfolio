Project Gecko: Your Ultimate Emissions Management Solution

GECkO, the Emissions Management System (EMS) app, empowers companies to accurately track, report, and reduce carbon emissions across their supply chains. Featuring an intuitive dashboard, GECkO provides real-time data visualization, detailed reporting, and actionable insights to help companies meet their sustainability goals. GECkO fosters a collaborative environment where companies can share best practices and success stories, driving towards a sustainable and net carbon zero future together.
