Project Hexle

Hexle: Unlimited is a code-breaking game where players crack color-coded clues to guess a hexadecimal number (0-F). With unlimited attempts, players aim to guess the correct number in the fewest tries. Compete with friends to climb the leaderboard and become the ultimate Hexle champion.

Key Features

  • Hexadecimal Puzzles: Crack codes using hexadecimal digits (0-F).
  • Unlimited Attempts: Guess as many times as needed, aiming for the fewest tries.
  • Leaderboard: Compete with friends and track your progress.
  • Logic & Deduction: Test and improve your problem-solving skills with each round.
