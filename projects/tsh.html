Project TSH

Project TSH aims to develop a system that streamlines the training and development process for TSH, a manufacturing company. The system will enhance the efficiency of managing employee training, based on a thorough analysis of current processes and input from the Training and Development HR department.

Key Features

  • Training Management: Simplifies the tracking and management of employee training.
  • Data-Driven Insights: Provides analytics to improve training efficiency.
  • Customizable Solutions: Tailored to the specific needs of the organization.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Intuitive design for easy navigation and usability.
